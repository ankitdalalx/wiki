Tiger Shroff is a famous Indian actor, dancer and martial artist. He is the son of popular Bollywood actor Jackie Shroff. He became an overnight star with his debut movie, Heropanti, which was a huge commercial success at the box office. Let’s check out some interesting facts about Tiger Shroff.

Biography/Wiki

Tiger Shroff was born on 2 March 1990 (age 33 years; as of 2023) in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. He was very active in his childhood and began to take Martial Arts training right from the age of 4. Having an actor father and producer mother, there was always a pinch of acting into his blood. He admired Hrithik Roshan and was deeply inclined to become a successful actor like him.

Tiger Shroff’s Childhood Picture

In the year 2002, Tiger got his first break, in movie Heropanti (2014). The movie did very well at the box office and his performance was also praised by the viewers. He worked very hard for the movie and spent a total of 3 years to get trained for it.

Shroff next appeared in the action drama Baaghi (2016) opposite Shraddha Kapoor, which turned out to be a hit at the box office and grossed ₹1 billion worldwide. In the same year, Tiger also appeared in Remo D’Souza’s- A Flying Jatt opposite alongside the much-talented actress Jacqueline Fernandez. In 2018, he again rocked the cinemas by making a comeback with the sequel of Baaghi- Baaghi 2, along with his alleged girlfriend Disha Patani.

Tiger is now all set to star in Punit Malhotra’s Student Of The Year 2 (2019), which is a sequel to, Student of the Year released in 2012.

Physical Appearance

Tiger Shroff is a fitness enthusiast and owns a strongly built physique of 44 inches chest, 30 inches waist, and 15 inches biceps. The brown-eyed actor is around 5’ 9” in height and weighs around 70 kg.

Family

Parents & Siblings

Tiger Shroff’s father Jackie Shroff is a famous Bollywood actor while his mother Ayesha Dutt is a producer. His sister Krishna Shroff is also a producer by profession.

Relationships/Affairs

Disha Patani

Tiger is alleged to be dating the Baaghi 2 actress Disha Patani. In July 2022, a rumour started doing the rounds on the internet that Tiger and Disha had parted ways after dating each other for several years. While giving the reason behind the breakup, a source claimed that the couple decided to part ways after Disha asked Tiger if they should get married, and Tiger replied that he was not ready for a matrimonial bond. Deesha Dhanuka

In August 2023, reports emerged that Tiger was in a relationship with Deesha Dhanuka, a senior professional who works in a production house. The pair had reportedly been dating for around a year and a half. According to sources, Deesha often provided input on scripts for Tiger, who in turn guided her fitness regimen. Tiger’s family was said to be fond of Deesha, and their relationship was no secret to those close to them; [2]The Times of India however, when Tiger Shroff was asked about his relationship with Deesha, he replied,

I thought I was linked to somebody else a couple of months ago, but no, I’ve been single for the past two years.”

Career

Tiger was an average student since his childhood. He did his schooling from Besant Montessori School, Juhu, Mumbai and American School of Bombay, Mumbai. He then went on to Amity University, Noida to pursue his further education but couldn’t complete it.

He was offered the lead role in the remake of the TV show Fauji in 2009 but he refused to accept it as he only wanted to concentrate on movies.

He was finally signed on by Sajid Nadiadwala to star in his Debut movie Heropanti in the year 2012. Heropanti was a great success on the box office. Tiger received the love of critics and the public for his extraordinary dancing skills and stunts performed in the movie.

Controversy

On 2 June 2021, Tiger Shroff, along with Disha Patani, was booked by the Bandra police for violating the COVID-19 lockdown norms. The police registered a case under Section 188 of the Indian Penal Code (disobeying the order of public servant) against the actors and their driver as they were out on the road after 2 pm, despite a curfew was imposed in the area. [3]Hindustan Times

Awards

Tiger has been entitled to various awards for his movie Heropanti. He received the award for Superstar of Tomorrow at Stardust Awards in 2014. He also received the award for the most entertaining actor at BIG Star Entertainment Awards.

Tiger Shroff holding IIFA Award

Tiger bagged an award for Star Debut of the Year (2015) at IIFA Awards. He has also been awarded the Most Promising Newcomer award at Life Ok Screen Awards for Heropanti.
